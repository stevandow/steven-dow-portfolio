Product
Designer.

I'm a Product Designer with more than 20 years of design experience.

I partner with teams around the globe on design engagements ranging from single app design to enterprise-scale implementation, creating unique customer experiences for millions of users.

I make sure my concepts and design solutions are well thought through, and based on thorough research. I'm a good listener, I make sure I understand the business requirements, and I'm strong at aligning with stakeholders.
