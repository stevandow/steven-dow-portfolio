Client

SBS Broadcasting

Role

Senior UI/UX Designer

Methods

Design sprints, wireframing, contextual inquiry, design thinking, concept development, concept sketching,

Solutions

We undertook a complete responsive redesign with an updated brand, and a customer-centric experience. We created a new IA with content strategy, and updated interaction models.

About the project

KIJK is a Dutch ad-supported streaming service offering a selection of TV shows, clips, and movies on kijk.nl. KIJK subscribers can access episodes from SBS6, Veronica, Net5, and SBS9 KIJK offers a monthly subscription fee service available on computers and laptops, smartphones, tablets, TVs, and other devices.

Visit Website

My role

I was responsible for doing competitive research and analysis of similar services, branding, UX design, visual design and art direction for the responsive website. I colaberated together with a Visual Designer and Interaction Designer from unitid. They were responsible for designing the iPad and iPhone app.

The challenge

I was hired to rethink the existing online service from KIJK and develop a new brand experience for their responisve website.

Old KIJK website

Research & Competitive Analysis

There were similar services out there such as Videoland, Pathe and NPO. I signed up for every service to research their user flows, to understand their business model and see how they compared to the concept of KIJK.

Style tile

I created a couple of style tiles and an option of fonts together with the Visual Designer. This was done to give the stakeholders an idea of which direction we could go.

Sketching

I always start my projects with sketching. I played with different design flows and interaction possibilities, and I got rid of the ideas that didn’t work for me.

Wireframing and site map

Before I started wireframing I created a site map, this gave me a good overview of the structure and complexity of the website. The Interaction Designer was responsible for creating the wireframes for the iPad and mobile app, I used her wireframes as a referance for designing the website.

Site map

Desktop website

User Interface

We created a GUI kit so that it was easier to mockup all the screens that we needed. This also helped speed up the process and keep design consistent.

The results

+200 pages online, a brand new design that is simple engaging, and last but not least, a satisfied customer!
