Reimagining
time entry
for employees.

Rapid prototyping

Designing
an intelligent
time card App.

For operations, it’s challenging to get 100% submission of time entries on time, which negatively impacts the business’s ability to measure performance.

Gathering
insights.

Using interviews, we were able to gather insight into the process and sentiments associated with time submission.

While essential to the business, there were inconsistent perspectives on how time should be tracked and submitted. In addition, time submission was often seen as a distraction from customer engagements.

With all of the information already available, employees wondered why certain processes were not automated and intuitive allowing them to focus on the immediate demands of their role.

Key Performance Indicators.

Design a tool to intelligently track employee time. Support the working styles of employees by allowing multiple methods to track time. Automate mundane tasks and enable employees to focus on their customer.

Rapid
prototyping.

To bring our idea to life we started with wireframing, design and prototyping fast using rapid prototyping method.

We designed low to high-fidelity designs iteratively.

User
testing.

To take the guesswork out of our design decisions we validated them with real users in our office and used a remote user research platform to validate it with our partners in other regions.
